Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 5, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how inQRedible ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

inQRedible is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Content you create: The text, URLs, or data you enter to generate QR codes is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Images you add: Any logos or images you add to your QR codes remain on your device and are not uploaded to any server.
  • Purchase information: If you make in-app purchases, Apple processes the transaction. We receive confirmation of your purchase status but not your payment details.

How We Use Information

We use information only to:

  • Provide the QR code generation functionality
  • Verify your purchase status for premium features
  • Improve our app's performance and user experience

Data Storage

All QR codes you create and any images you add are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not backed up to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For in-app purchases and subscription management
  • Apple StoreKit: For processing transactions

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We do not collect personal information to share.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information for us to delete or provide. All data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us
